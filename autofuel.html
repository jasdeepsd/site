Autofuel Mechanical|Electrical

Project Description

Cartesian Robot that is designed to automate the refuelling process of a vehicle. The system can locate the fuel door on the vehicle, open/close the fuel door, remove/replace the fuel cap and insert a fuel nozzle into the inlet. Capstone project and is in progress.
